What's athena square?

We are building a community of working professionals, graduate alumni, students, and prospective students. Our community gives people a space to talk about their careers, form connections with like-minded peers, get mentorship from industry experts, and participate in recruiting opportunities!

Networking

Join us and get access to our slack community. You can share interesting articles, exciting events, and useful resources. This is also your place to seek advice, ask career questions, and get peer feedback.

Personalized Mentorship

Get personalized mentorship from our mentors. You only pay per session and at the price that meets your budget!

Anytime, Anywhere

Take session online at the time and place that suits you.

How does it work?

1. CHOOSE YOUR MENTOR
Get to know your mentor through slack and open zoom sessions and read reviews from other students.
2. BOOK YOUR SESSION
Schedule personalized or group sessions at a time and date that suit you.
3. START YOUR JOURNEY
Connect with your Mentor via video chat, and let the mentoring begin!
